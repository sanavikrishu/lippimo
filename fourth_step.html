Now for anything, we always procrastinate.
This page is to stop you from procrastinating.

1. Prioritize your tasks and responsibilities.

2. Divide the task into smaller parts.

3. Stay away from all-or-none thinking.

4. Take breaks in scheduled increments.

5. Support yourself with like-minded people.

6. Celebrate small wins.

7. Give yourself soft deadlines for completing tasks.

8. Change your mindset.

9. Define the outcome you want.

10. Focus on all the benefits.

By doing this, you will be able to overcome procrastination. Trust me.
