Transform Through Systems

To become a person who achieves their goals and to achieve your goals, you must become that person first. By that I mean, make that person a part of your identity, make their habits your habits. For example, if you want to be a valedictorian, finalize a valedictorian's habits. What would a valedictorian do? Maybe wake up at 6:30, go to the gym, and then start studying efficiently using study methods and actually liking studying, stuff like that. That's what a valedictorian would do.

So, you start becoming a valedictorian by doing that stuff. And then you're guaranteed to become a valedictorian. Those things are called systems. As the famous book Atomic Habits says: "You won't rise to the level of your goals, you'll fall to the level of your systems."

Hence, this page is to transform who you are by setting your systems.
