Consistency Tips

1. Set Clear Goals

Know exactly what you want to achieve. Make your goals simple and easy to follow.

2. Build Good Habits

Create small routines you can do every day. Start easy and slowly add harder tasks.

3. Use Reminders

Keep track of what you do. Use alarms, calendars, or notes to remind you.

4. Share Your Goals

Tell someone about your goals. Check in with them for support and motivation.

5. Focus on Doing, Not Perfect

It’s okay if it’s not perfect. Celebrate small wins to stay motivated.

6. Face Challenges

Take action even if it’s hard. Keep going even if you make mistakes.
